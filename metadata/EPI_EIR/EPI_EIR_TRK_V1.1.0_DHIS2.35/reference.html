Independently from the stage of roll-out, the average age at enrollment should not variate much as the EPI has a relatively strict schedule based on the age of the child. The age might be higher at the beginning of the program as it might be trying to catch up on older children, but the age should then go steadily down though time as the program embeds in the health activities at PoC.
